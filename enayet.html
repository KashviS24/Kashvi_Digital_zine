ENAYET

Enayet Kabir (eh-nah-yeth) is a Bangladesh-born artist based in the United States of America. He is known to produce music that can be categorized as dance/electronic, under the name Enayet.

He is the co-founder of slink NYC which is a NYC night and label for slinked-out dance music and slippery club rhythms.
